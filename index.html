Private art classes to suit your needs

Young Artists' Place is a small studio in Burnaby, BC that offers personalized private and semi-private art classes. Whether you're an aspiring anime artist, a student with special needs, or a pair of siblings looking for a more focused learning environment, classes here are tailored to your specific needs.

The studio takes a modern approach by offering instruction in new and contemporary mediums, like digital art and comics. A new generation of young artists is emerging, and art doesn't always have to be hanging in a gallery anymore!

Drawing & Painting

Private, personalized drawing and painting classes that focuses specifically on 2D mediums. We'll use inks, paints, and a variety of tools to get away from just regular pencil drawing.

Digital Art

Ideal for teens or adults who are interested in anime, illustration, or comics, and who already have a good grasp of drawing fundamentals. No prior experience needed!
